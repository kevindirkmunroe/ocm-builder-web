For making transparent images:
1. Screenshot print to approx 840 x 840px. The screenshot is a PNG image that is < 1MB, compared to
   3+MB of original image
2. Save image, then upload to : https://tech-lagoon.com/imagechef/en/white-to-transparent.html
3. Transform white pixels to transparent, then save image in src/assets.
